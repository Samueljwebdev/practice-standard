# Pricing — The Practice Standard

The Practice Standard is a UK healthcare job board. Practices pay to post jobs; professionals always apply for free.

## Pay-Per-Listing

- **Price:** £149 + VAT per listing
- **Duration:** 30 days active
- **Includes:** Unlimited applications, dashboard management, JobPosting schema markup
- **Best for:** Practices with occasional hiring needs

## Practice Pro (Monthly Subscription)

- **Price:** £249 + VAT per month
- **Listings:** Unlimited job postings
- **Includes:** Priority placement, all Pay-Per-Listing features, account management
- **Best for:** Growing practices or those with ongoing recruitment

## Professionals (Candidates)

- **Price:** Free — always
- **Account required:** Yes (free to create)
- **Application fee:** None
- **Features:** Job search, saved jobs, application tracking dashboard

## Notes

- All prices are in GBP and exclude VAT
- VAT at the standard UK rate (20%) is added at checkout
- Payments processed securely via Stripe
- Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time
- No contract, no setup fee
