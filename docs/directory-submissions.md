## Standard listing copy

### 50-word version (for short-form directories)
The Practice Standard is the UK's specialist job board for regulated private healthcare practices. Covering all 88 regulated professions — veterinary, optometry, aesthetics, physiotherapy, private medical, dental, and more — across all 12 UK regions. Practices post from £149. Candidates always free. No agency fees.

### 150-word version (for association directories and partner pages)
The Practice Standard (thepracticestandard.co.uk) is the UK's only job board built exclusively for regulated private healthcare practices.

Unlike general job boards such as Indeed or Reed, every role on The Practice Standard is a private practice position — no NHS listings, no unfiltered general applicants. Every candidate is registered with their relevant professional body (RCVS, GOC, GDC, HCPC, NMC, or GMC).

We cover all 88 regulated healthcare professions across all 12 UK regions, including veterinary, optometry, aesthetics and medical aesthetics, physiotherapy and allied health, private GP and medical, and dental.

Practices post roles from £149 +VAT per listing, or £249/month for unlimited listings with Practice Pro. Candidates can search, browse, and apply for free — always.

The Practice Standard is an alternative to expensive specialist boards and recruitment agencies — at a fraction of the cost.
