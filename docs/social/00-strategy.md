# The Practice Standard — Social Content Library (104 posts)

A ready-to-post library for LinkedIn + Instagram. Post manually. Mix and reorder freely.

## What the research says (so we post what actually works)
- **LinkedIn:** carousels/documents get ~6.6% engagement vs ~0.9% for text — and **recruitment/staffing is the single highest-engagement niche** (18–25% reply rates). Hooks must open with a **number, a contrarian line, or tension**. The algorithm rewards **dwell time**, so value-dense posts win.
- **Instagram:** **Reels drive reach** (keep ~60–70% of IG output video); **carousels drive saves** (114% more engagement than single images; checklists are "save magnets"). **Saves + DM shares are weighted 3–5× over likes.** Carousel/Reel covers must answer *"is this for me?"* and *"what will I get?"* in under 10 words.

## Audiences
- **Practices (buyers):** owners, principals, practice managers, clinic directors. Pain: agency fees (15–25% of salary), Indeed floods (68% fail screening), no private-practice board.
- **Professionals (supply):** vets, nurses, physios, optometrists, aesthetic practitioners, private GPs. Pain: "everything is NHS," scattered across 5 sites.

## Suggested cadence
- **6-month engine:** 4 posts/week (2 LinkedIn + 2 Instagram) = 104 posts.
- Or **1 year** at 2/week total. Best slots: **Tue–Thu, 7–9am** (LinkedIn), **lunch + 7–9pm** (Instagram).
- Always reply to every comment in the first hour (dwell + algorithm boost).

## Format key

- Colours: navy `#0F3D3E` (deep teal-navy), mint `#A8D5CC`, off-white `#F2F4F3`.
- Big bold sans headline, lots of whitespace, one stat per slide, the cross logo bottom-corner.
- Carousels: 1080×1350 (4:5). Reels/Stories: 1080×1920 (9:16).
